## Various Copenhagen alternatives
The following suggestions usually involves a lot of walking, so make sure to wear comfortable shoes and bring a bottle of water. Some of the attractions may have entrance fees, so it's a good idea to check their websites for the latest information on opening hours and ticket prices. Enjoy exploring Copenhagen!


### Round Tower
Round tower (Rundetårn) is a unique historical site in Copenhagen. Contains tetragrammaton on the front (you will have to look straight up to see it).  
GPS coordinates: 55°40'53"N 12°34'33"E (In GoogleMaps search for: 55.6813, 12.5759)


### Netto bådene
A cheap and fun way to explore the canals of Copenhagen is by taking a ride on the Netto bådene, which are small boats that operate on a hop-on-hop-off basis. They offer a great way to see the city from the water and visit various attractions along the canals.  
Webpage: https://www.havnerundfart.dk  
Address: Nyhavn 2, 1051 København  
GPS coordinates: 55°40'49"N 12°35'19"E (In GoogleMaps search for: 55.6802, 12.5886)


### The Little Mermaid
The Little Mermaid statue is one of Copenhagen's most famous landmarks. It is inspired by Hans Christian Andersen's fairy tale and depicts a mermaid sitting on a rock by the waterside. The statue is a symbol of the city and attracts millions of visitors each year.  
GPS coordinates: 55°41'34"N 12°35'57"E (In GoogleMaps search for: 55.6929, 12.5992)


### Tivoli Gardens

There is an entrance fee to access the park, and it is recommended to check the official website for opening hours and ticket prices.

## Public transportation

### Train
If you want to travel between e.g. Holbaek and Copenhagen by train, you can use the services provided by DSB, the Danish State Railways. DSB operates regular train services between Holbaek and Copenhagen, making it a convenient option for travelers. You can check the train schedules and book tickets on their official website.
Please make sure to activate your ticket before boarding the train, as failure to do so may result in a fine. You can activate your ticket using the DSB app or at the ticket machines located at the train station. It's important to have a valid ticket for your journey, so please ensure that you have it ready before boarding the train.  
Website: https://www.dsb.dk/en/

### Subway/Metro/S-train/Bus
Copenhagen has an efficient public transportation system that includes a metro, S-trains, and buses. The metro is a convenient way to get around the city, with several lines connecting different neighborhoods and major attractions. The S-trains are also a popular choice for traveling within the city and to nearby suburbs. Buses provide additional coverage and can take you to areas that are not served by the metro or S-trains. You can use the Rejseplanen website or app to plan your journey and find the best routes and schedules for public transportation in Copenhagen.
There is a Rejsekort app that can be installed on your phone, which allows you to easily pay for public transportation in Copenhagen. The Rejsekort is a contactless travel card that can be used on buses, trains, and the metro. You can top up your card with credit and use it to tap in and out when boarding and exiting public transportation. It's a convenient and cost-effective way to get around the city.
Please remember to check-in/check-out when using public transportation with the Rejsekort, as failing to do so may result in being charged a higher fare.  
Website: https://www.rejsekort.dk/?sc_lang=en
